Biryani (noun): An Indian delicacy, comprising of heavily spiced rice and a choice of meat or fish

With an impressive average order of 200 Biriyani's Per Minute (BPM), it can be deduced that India has a robust market for this beloved dish.
115 (BPM) in 2021 and 137 (BPM) in 2022.

Fact: Veg biryani is a myth . Without meat, it's simply "spiced rice".
